WiiNet

Powered by WiiLink

WiiNet is a project started by me in my free time as a website to utilize the Internet Channel's tricks and features to revive older abilities of it such as video playback and games. Recently, this was project was taken under WiiLink and is now owned by them. As the original project owner, I have high regards in their skill for the project from seeing what they've done with Wii No Ma, and the Digicam Print Channel. On the topic of Wii No Ma, you can check out the WiiNet demo from the WiiNet Room in the Rooms category if you're interested in giving it a test drive.
